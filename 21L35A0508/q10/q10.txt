NUMBER PATTERN

For every n value, print the respective pattern of n rows.

Input 
. First line contains t denoting the number of test cases,
. Next t lines contain n .

Output
. For every test case, print the n rows pattern separated by the next line.

Constraints 
1 < t < 10
1 < n < 100

Sample Input
2
3
5
Sample Output
1
3 4
7 6 12

1
3 4
7 6 12
8 15 13 18
12 28 14 24 24
