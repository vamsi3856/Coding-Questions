Last-Minute Assignment Rescue

Tom forgot to complete an assignment his teacher gave him, but he arrived at school early. Now, he only has a few minutes before school starts. His assignment is to perform operations on a combination of numbers, (), and []. He needs to add the numbers in (), and multiply the numbers in [].
Tom asked his senior help. Imagine you are Tom's senior and help him solve his assignment. 

Input Format:
First line contains an integer 'n' representing the length of a string
Second line contains the string

Output Format:
Resultant output is an integer value

Constraints:
1<=n<=10^2

Sample input:
15
126(12[23(12)])

Sample Output:
12612
