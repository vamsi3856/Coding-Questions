5.Find leaders
One day teacher came to class and want to make leaders in the class. All N students are standing in a sequence. Now it’s teachers turn to decide the leader. To become a leader, his / her height is strictly greater than the all the elements on the right hand side. Teacher got a call and gave this task to the class CR. You are the class CR and make the leaders from the class.
Note:
•	Right most person is always a leader
•	Output format should be in the same as the given sequence.
Constraints:
1<=N<=10^4
1<=array[i]<=10^4
Input format:
First line consists of a integer N
Second line consists of array of size N
Output format:
Array of leaders
Sample input:
6
6 7 4 2 5 3
Sample output:
7 5 3
